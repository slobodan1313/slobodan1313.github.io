ADI PunchOut + Purchase Order Flow

Complete Business Process Overview

What is PunchOut?

PunchOut is an ecosystem that enables ADI Global buyers to access their predefined catalogs for e-shopping. When the buyer decides to purchase, the cart seamlessly returns to the buyer's procurement system for approval. After approval, the Purchase Order is stored in both the buyer's ERP and ADI's P21 ERP, then flows through our systems to create Sales Orders in P21 for fulfillment.
