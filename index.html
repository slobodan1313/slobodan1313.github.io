Key Learnings & Best Practices

‚úì Discovery-First Methodology Validated

Investing in comprehensive discovery of Greenwing "black box" and three-phase flow prevented costly rework. Staying in exploration phase vs. jumping to solutions proved critical for complex integrations.

‚úì Evidence-Based Documentation Essential
