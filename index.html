Key Learnings & Best Practices

‚úì Discovery-First Methodology Validated

Investing in comprehensive discovery of Greenwing "black box" and three-phase flow prevented costly rework. Staying in exploration phase vs. jumping to solutions proved critical for complex integrations.

‚úì Evidence-Based Documentation Essential

Facts-only approach and rejection of assumptions created accountability framework that enabled 81-day blockage resolution. Email threads and Jira traceability prevented vendor finger-pointing.

‚úì Single-Point Dependencies Are Critical Risks

Dependency on Jason Capshaw (ERP architect) created project bottleneck. Future projects require knowledge transfer plans and backup resources for critical technical roles.

‚úì Test Scenarios Without User Stories Limit UAT

Developing test scenarios without formal acceptance criteria limits UAT to functional validation vs. business requirement validation. Process improvement needed for future sprints.
